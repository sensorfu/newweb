About us

We enable organizations to proactively detect even the most intricate misconfigurations before they compromise operations.

Going back to the basics of cyber security

SensorFu’s philosophy is rooted in the idea of going back to the basics of cyber security. Building and maintaining secure business-critical networks can feel overwhelming in an increasingly complex world. At SensorFu, we understand that challenge. We're here to bring you simplicity, proactive visibility, and confidence in your network defenses.

Our product SensorFu Beacon is a solution for proactive and continuous validation of network isolation and segmentation. It is used in business-critical environments, such as industrial OT and ICS networks, SCADA systems and other environments which demand reliability of network segmentation without risking the availability of operations.

Founded in Finland, we are driven by a hacker mindset rooted in transparency, innovation, trust and simplicity in design. We believe in the universal language of straightforward security.
