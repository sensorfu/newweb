Always know if your network leaks

Designed for customers who depend on network segmentation

The core security control, especially in OT environments, is network segmentation. It’s essential, yet often uncertain. You may call it isolation, segregation, partition, sandboxing or air-gapping - but do you know if it works?

We’ve found out that over 80% of the organisations that have deployed Beacons into their live production networks have found surprises in their network segmentation. Knowing the leaks is the first step to fixing the leaks.

Firewall rules, routing, VPNs, switch VLANs, cabling - it is impossible to know that everything is working correctly everywhere, all the time! With SensorFu Beacon, the verification of your most important security control is straightforward and continuous.

Continuous Validation, Minimal Friction

Beacons are intentionally lightweight. They run silently in the background, trying to reach their Home, sending an escape test packet once every second. The escape packets are attempts to communicate through isolation boundaries using multiple methods over IPv4 and IPv6 networks. If a Beacon reaches Home, the system alerts instantly, no delays, no overload.

Deploy Where It Matters

From energy substations and oil refineries to food and beverage production and logistics automation, Beacon adapts to complex environments. Use it across multiple sites or mission-critical network segments to continuously verify that your segmentation policies are effective.

Rather than finding 300 potential issues in one location, test one important security control in 300 locations

Quick and easy deployment

Beacons can be deployed as Linux or Windows applications, Docker containers, virtual machines, or dedicated devices. SensorFu Beacon integrates seamlessly with most SIEM systems, IDS solutions and NDR providers.

Beacon Application

The Beacon Application is easy to deploy in existing environments. You can use management tools such as Microsoft System Center, Group Policy Objects (GPO), Puppet, Ansible, and others. Simply integrate the Beacon Application into your automation workflows to gain full visibility into the effectiveness of your network isolation.

Beacon Virtual

Launch virtual Beacons inside your data centers to see if they can reach their Home. Download the Beacon Virtual Machine in standard OVA format from Beacon Home, place the VM in the appropriate network, and you’re ready to go

Beacon Device

Use Beacon Devices to test network isolation in on-premises environments. Leave them behind, move them around—deploy them anywhere. Compatible with Raspberry Pi*. Simply download the image from Beacon Home, copy it to an SD card, and you’re ready to deploy the Beacon Device to your network. *Raspberry Pi is a trademark of the Raspberry Pi Foundation
